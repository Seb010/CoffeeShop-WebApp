Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What are your opening hours?

We are open Monday to Friday from 7 AM to 7 PM and Saturday to Sunday from 8 AM to 5 PM.

Where can I find you?

We are located at 123 Road, London, E7 F4Y. You can get in touch with us at info@coffeeshop.com or call us on +44 123 456 789.

Do you offer vegan or dairy-free options?

Yes, we offer a variety of vegan and dairy-free milk alternatives, as well as plant-based pastries.

Do you have Wi-Fi available?

Yes, we provide free Wi-Fi for our customers. Feel free to bring your laptop and enjoy a cup of coffee while you work!

Do you sell gift cards?

Yes, we offer gift cards that can be purchased at our shop.

Can I order for collection?

Yes, we have a new service which allows our customers to order for a collection for a date and time.

Can I bring my pet?

We love pets! You are welcome to bring your well-behaved dogs to our outdoor seating area.

Are your coffee beans ethically sourced?

Yes, we are committed to sourcing our coffee beans from sustainable and ethical farms. We prioritize fair trade practices.
